Ingredients you will need:

  • Low moisture whole milk mozzarella cheese
  • Fontina
  • A good Parmesan or
  • An aged sharp cheddar

Instructions:

  1. Pick the cheese(s) you want, mozzarella should be the base cheese.
  2. Grate all your cheeses.
  3. Mix all your cheeses together in the ratios you want.
  4. Put your custom blend into a bowl and set aside.
