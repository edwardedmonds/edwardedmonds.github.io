Ingredients you will need:

  • 4 1/2 cups all purpose or bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups of water

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl combine flour, salt, and yeast.
  2. Mix in water and mix with your hands until everything is combined into a rough ball. It doesn't need to be perfect.
  3. Cover and let sit for 30 to 45 minutes to allow the dough to hydrate.
  4. After that time has passed, uncover your bowl and knead the dough a few times recover.
  5. Let sit until it is about doubled in size.
  6. Cut the dough in half and save the other half by putting it in a sealed bowl in the freezer.
  7. With the other half form back into a ball and let it rest.
  8. When the dough is rested and looks relaxed it is ready to use.
