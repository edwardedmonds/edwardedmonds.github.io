Ingredients you will need:

  • Canned Peeled Whole Tomatoes
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Sugar

  1. Open your can of tomatoes and put a strainer into a bowl and pour the can of tomatoes through the strainer.
  2. Crush the tomatoes with your hands and pull out any green bits.
  3. Put your tomatoes from the strainer into a bowl and use the left over tomato juices in the other bowl to create a sauce consistency you like.
  4. Add salt, pepper, sugar, and crushed red pepper flakes to taste.
  5. Add a splash of olive oil. Olive oil is a must!
  6. Mix everything together cover and let chill in the fridge. You do not need to cook your sauce the canned tomatoes are already cooked. We just need to let the other ingredients marinate with the tomatoes.
